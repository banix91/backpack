Welcome to Mercy Backpacks

Following the command of Jesus…“Go and do likewise.”

In 2019, there were over 700 shootings in Baltimore City.

The opportunity to minster to victims of gun violence is great. It also provides the faith community a great opportunity to do whatever they can to show the love of Christ to victims of gun violence. We all can play a part helping those in need.

Demonstrating the heart of Christ

The story of the Good Samaritan found in Luke 10:30-37 shows all of us the heart of Christ when it comes to serving those that are victims of crime. Then Jesus said to him, “Go and do likewise.” What an amazing command from Jesus. When we stand with and encourage victims of gun violence, we clearly demonstrate the heart of Christ by showing mercy to those that have been wounded physically and emotionally.

Our ministry goal is simple…

…to follow the command of Jesus, “Go and do likewise.” Mercy Backpacks are filled with much needed toiletries and backpacks to shock trauma patients preparing to return home from treatment at the University of Maryland Medical Center.Our partnership with the Violence Prevention Program at the Shock Trauma Center has made it possible for Mercy Backpacks to be distributed to their patients returning home.

Who We Are

Mercy Backpacks is a ministry to victims of gun violence. Since 2017, we have reached out to victims of gun violence through out partnership with the Violence Prevention Program at the Shock Trauma Center University of Maryland Medical Center.We have distributed over 130 Mercy Backpacks to victims of gun violence.
