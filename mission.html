Mercy Backpacks Mission

"Be Kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other just as Christ God forgave you."

Ephesians 4:32

What does it mean to have compassion?

"It is something that we at Mercy Backpack stand on. Compassion is the ability to give and show sincere empathy for one another. Often times we judgement as a first act of a person but compassion allows you to mimic Christ in our lives in the lives of others."
